My Subject Work

The subjects I take at Sixth Form are Computer Science, Geography and Maths. Both Computer Science and Geography involve coursework, whereas Maths is 100% exam based.

Computer Science

My computer science course involves a theory exam, a programming exam, and coursework. For the programming exam we will use the programming language Python.

Geography

My Geography course involves two exams, a physical geography paper and a human geography paper, as well as a 4000 word coursework document.

Maths

The maths A-Level includes 6 modules. Three modules were undertaken in Year 12 (Core 1, Core 2, and Mechanics) and three in Year 13 (Core 3, Core 4, and Statistics).
